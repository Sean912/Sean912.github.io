What is a Shiba?

The shiba is one of the native dog breeds in Japan,
this is by far one of the most popular dogs found in Japan, It is known for its intelligence, small upright ears and agility. The shiba was used as a hunter to go after boars and birds, it also has a thick coat of fur to withstand the cold temperature in Japan.

Doge meme

The doge meme became popular during 2013, the meme consists of a picture of a cute shiba inu with words around the picture, the words around the image have the comic sans font and its also in broken english. The meme picture has gone less viral but the words "much wow" and "such amaze" are still used widely.
